About Us

The Gamma Pi Chapter of Zeta Tau Alpha, located at the University of Georgia, is one of the top fraternity chapters in the nation. Since being chartered in 1949, the chapter has been committed to excellence, represnted by their number of awards of the highest honor. The chapter encompasses over two hundered strong, empowered women all seeking involvement in both their university and their community. It prides itself on academic achievement, leadership, involvement, and service. Within the Gamma Pi Chapter, members are guaranteed to discover the spirit of love, sentiments, and friendship.




Our Philanthropy

Within Zeta Tau Alpha, love is essentially the greatest of all things. To share their love for the Athens community, the Gamma Pi Chapter, located at the University of Georgia, puts great emphasis on their philanthropy, breast cancer awareness. Each year in April, the sorority chapter hosts a community-wide fundraising event called "Diamond Challenge." Teams made up of ten to twelve men compete against eachother in games of softball, all while raising money for this wonderful cause. Through extended commitment, this past year, the University of Georgia Zeta Tau Alpha Chapter raised over $100,000. This page is to highlight and recap their great accomplishes. All of us have been affected by cancer in one way or another, but together, we can make a difference!



How to Donate

Breast Cancer is currently the most commonly diagnosed cancer in the world. In fact, about 1 in 8 women will develop breast cancer over the course of their life. In Zeta Tau Alpha, we strive to do everything we can to bring awareness to this medical disease. To do this, we need your help! In order to accomplish the high fundraising goals that Gamma Pi has set, the chapter needs many monetary donations. If interested in donating, visit our website linked at the bottom of this webpage. Knowing we are making a difference in many lives is something the Gamma Pi Chapter of Zeta Tau Alpha takes pride in. Join in on this pride and make a difference today!
